About Dastoor-e-Rah

Dastoor-e-Rah is an awareness initiative designed to highlight the importance of following traffic laws in order to create safer roads and a more responsible society. Traffic accidents are one of the leading causes of injuries and fatalities worldwide, and many of these incidents occur due to negligence, lack of awareness, or disregard for road regulations.

Our project aims to educate people about safe driving habits, the value of discipline on the road, and the shared responsibility of drivers, pedestrians, and authorities in maintaining order and safety in public spaces.

Why Traffic Laws Are Important

Traffic laws exist to ensure safety, organization, and fairness on the road. Without clear rules, roads would become chaotic and dangerous for everyone.
Following traffic laws helps reduce accidents, protects lives, and ensures that emergency services can operate efficiently when needed.
Respecting traffic rules is also a sign of civic responsibility and respect for fellow citizens who share the road.

Essential Traffic Rules

  • Always obey traffic lights and road signs.
  • Wear seatbelts and helmets at all times.
  • Do not use mobile phones while driving.
  • Follow speed limits and drive responsibly.
  • Respect pedestrian crossings.
  • Maintain safe distance between vehicles.
